# Contributing to Airbnb Arbitrage Ottawa

We welcome contributions to help streamline operations, improve automation, and refine our business strategies.

## How to Contribute

1.  **Documentation**: Improvements to the business plan, regulatory research, or financial models.
2.  **Automation**: Scripts, workflows (n8n), or prompts for guest communication.
3.  **Market Research**: Data on neighborhoods, pricing trends, or competitor analysis.

## Pull Request Process

1.  Ensure any new documents are placed in the appropriate folder (`/documents`, etc.).
2.  Update the `README.md` if your change introduces new folders or tools.
3.  Describe your changes clearly in the PR description.

## Code of Conduct

Please keep all interactions professional and focused on the success of the venture.
